Blue Sky Broadcast

thoughtbot client project - March 2013

Redesign and rebuild Blue Sky Broadcasts existing business to business product.

The Team

Two rails developers and a project manager from the Blue Sky team, two rails developers from the thoughtbot team and myself as the designer.

My role as the designer was to lead the interaction design, user experice desing, interface design, product design, conduct research and user testing, impliment the application UI and lead the develpers so that any feature they work on were already implmented on the front end. I also worked in cordination with the Project manager to lead the direction of the redesign/rebuild.

Develop Understanding

  • Understand the solutions, values and outcomes the existing application delivers.

  • Understand the scope of the existing functionality, what works, what doesn't and why.

  • Understand the existing customers, their needs and how the current product fulfills or falls short of meeting those needs.

  • Understand the vision for the product and business so the rebuild can help facilitate the realization of future goals such as delivery of new functionality and acquisition of new customers from untapped markets.

To develop these necessary understandings I conducted interviews with: Blue Sky product managers, the Blue Sky CEO, the Blue Sky Sales and Marketing teams, Blue Sky engineers supporting the legacy app, internal "Platform Admins" who administer Blue Sky client accounts. I also studied as many existing client accounts as I could get access to. I was not allowed to interview or conduct tests with existing Blue Sky clients, but doing so would have been extreamly valuable.

The Legacy App

Blue Sky Broadcast offeres a business to business product for various organizations in industries from architecture and insurace to physics and pharmaceuticals. Blue Sky captures event presentations and lectures, archives the content online and delivers online courses created from the captured content to facilitate contined education or certification and accreditation for the memebers of their clients organizations.
